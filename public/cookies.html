Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Banaras Yatra uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device when you visit websites. They help website features operate correctly, analyze site traffic, and save user inputs during navigations.

2. Cookies We Use

We use essential cookies to maintain functional services, such as:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary files that expire when you close your browser. We use these to remember your session on our Admin CRM page.
  • Analytical Cookies: Standard web analytics (such as Google Analytics) to help us analyze visitor statistics and page loads, helping us improve user experience.

3. Controlling Cookies

You can adjust your web browser settings to block or delete cookies entirely. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact certain interactive features on our website (like admin CRM access).
