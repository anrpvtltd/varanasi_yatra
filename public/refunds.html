Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

At Banaras Yatra, we strive to offer transparent and flexible cancellation options for all travelers planning their trip to Varanasi.

1. Standard Cancellation Timeline

  • 7 days or more before travel: Full 100% refund of the token booking advance.
  • Less than 7 days but more than 48 hours before travel: 50% refund of the token booking advance, or option to adjust 100% of the advance amount to a future travel date (valid for up to 12 months).
  • Less than 48 hours before travel / No Show: No refund of the token advance, as these funds are immediately locked with hotels and transport drivers to secure availability.

2. How to Request Cancellation

To request a cancellation, please email us directly at info.varanasi.yatra@gmail.com or text your booking coordinator on WhatsApp at +91-8149783494 with your booking confirmation name and dates.

3. Processing of Refunds

Approved refunds are processed via the original payment source (UPI/Bank Transfer) and will reflect in your account within 5 to 7 business days.

4. Changes and Rescheduling

Rescheduling requests made more than 3 days prior to your travel date are processed free of charge, subject to room availability at the booked hotels.
