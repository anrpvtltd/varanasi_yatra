Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Welcome to Banaras Yatra. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our practices with regard to your personal info, please contact us at info.varanasi.yatra@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out our booking enquiry forms on our website. This includes:

  • Personal details: Name, email address, telephone/mobile number, and travel date.
  • Travel details: Pickup location preference and number of travelers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information collected via our website for the following business purposes:

  • To process your booking request and contact you with customized travel quotes.
  • To coordinate transport, hotel reservations, and tour guide services.
  • To send you updates or respond to customer service enquiries.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share information with third-party service providers (such as hotels, transport operators, and local guides) specifically necessary to execute your travel bookings. We do not sell or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the services outlined in this privacy policy, comply with our legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

5. Your Consent
